About

I have built my career on developing people and organizations. An expert on people and culture, I have led the People function at two companies. I have consulted in McKinsey's Strategy and Organization practices; founded and led Google's central learning and development program; worked in talent management at Standard Chartered Bank; and built and led People and Organization Development and Public Affairs at Agoda.com, the Asia-based subsidiary of the Priceline-Booking Holdings group, the world's leading online travel platform, and led the People function at APL Group and learning and leadership development at Aegis Ventures.

My education includes an M.B.A. in strategic management and health care management from Wharton and a Ph.D. in the humanities from the University of Chicago. I have served on the faculties of Princeton University, U.S.C., Pomona College, Nanyang Business School, Yale-National University of Singapore College, and Hult International Business School. I am a graduate of the Hudson Institute of Coaching and am certified as a coach by the International Coaching Federation.
